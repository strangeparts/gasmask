Making DIY filters
Materials:
Activated carbon
Bottle from activated carbon
Lid from activated carbon
Cotton wool
12-20mm drill bit - as large as can fit on lid, and in drill chuck.
3mm drill bit
superglue/epoxy
hot glue

Steps:
empty carbon bottle
drill 3mm holes in the bottom
fill base with cotton wool
add carbon
add cotton wool again
drill hole in lid - as large as possible
glue lid to face mask using epoxy or superglue
hotglue the edge of the lid to improve seal and fix leaks
screw on bottle
